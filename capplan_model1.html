What is included in the cost of the asset?
›
The cost includes the purchase price plus any delivery, installation, or setup costs required to make the asset ready for use. Does not include ongoing operating costs.
?
How do I know if my project is eligible for an Investment Tax Credit?
›
ITC eligibility depends on your jurisdiction and the type of asset. In Canada, certain clean energy and R&D equipment qualify. If you're unsure, leave this at 0%.
?
How do I estimate economic life and salvage value?
›
Economic life is how long the asset will be useful to your business. Salvage value is what you expect to sell it for at the end. Conservative estimates are recommended.
?
What depreciation method should I use and what is the half-year rule?
›
Double-declining gives the largest early deductions. Straight-line spreads them evenly. Sum-of-years-digits is in between. The half-year rule halves the first year's deduction.
?
How do I calculate the required rate of return?
›
This is your cost of capital — the minimum return needed to justify the investment. Use the Cost of Capital Calculator below to estimate it using WACC or CAPM.
