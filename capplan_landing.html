How it works

From decision to answer
in three steps

CapPlan turns complex capital planning calculations into guided, plain-language analyses anyone can complete.

01
Identify your decision
Use the solution finder to identify which model addresses your specific decision. Business owners, financial managers, and individuals all face different questions — the solution finder routes you to the right tool instantly.
02
Enter your numbers
Each model walks you through the inputs with plain-language labels, guidance on where to find data, and built-in estimators for figures that are hard to know precisely. Load an example to see how it works before entering your own data.
03
Understand your answer
Results include a clear decision recommendation, sensitivity analysis showing how the answer changes under different assumptions, and detailed DCF workings you can print or share. Every result tells you not just what, but why.
